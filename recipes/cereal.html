Cereal

Description:

Cereal is a breakfast food made from grains like oats, wheat, rice, or corn. It's often consumed with milk or yogurt and can come in various forms, including flakes, puffs, or granules. Cereal can be plain or flavored with fruits, nuts, chocolate, or other additives, making it a versatile and convenient meal option.


Ingredients:

  • 30-45g cereal (any of your choosing)
  • 125ml of milk
  • You will also require a bowl and spoon for this recipe

Steps:

  1. Firstly, put your bowl and spoon onto the table and pour the cereal contents into the bowl.
  2. Finally, pour in the milk and enjoy!
